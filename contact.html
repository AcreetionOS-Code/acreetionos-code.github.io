Bug Reports & Feature Requests

The only official way to submit bug reports and feature requests is by creating a ticket in our Discord server. We do not accept bug reports on GitHub.

Why We Use Discord for Support

You might wonder why we handle all support through Discord. As a project with code hosted across multiple platforms (like GitHub and private servers) and a community that spans multiple different timezones, we've made a deliberate choice to centralize our core communications.

Using Discord as our central hub ensures that every piece of feedback—from bug reports to feature ideas—is seen, tracked, and discussed in one unified place. This prevents valuable community input from getting lost, keeps our small development team synchronized, and creates a single, vibrant community where users and developers can collaborate effectively.

By creating a ticket on Discord, you're directly entering our primary workspace, guaranteeing the fastest and most organized response. We appreciate you helping us keep our workflow streamlined!

About Our Communication Platforms

Discord's ID Policy Enforcement (March 2026): Discord is forcing all users to verify their identity with a government-issued ID starting in March 2026. We strongly disagree with this policy, as it compromises user privacy and creates barriers to communication. Many users are uncomfortable providing such sensitive personal information to a private corporation, and we believe this requirement is unnecessary and overreaching.

Our Response - Alternative Options: In response to Discord's ID policy, we've expanded our support infrastructure to include Matrix (a decentralized, open-source messaging protocol), Stoat (an open-source project management platform), and Root (a privacy-focused Discord alternative). These alternatives allow community members to communicate with us without being forced to surrender their personal identification documents. We believe in protecting our users' privacy and providing options that respect individual choice.

Current Discord Status: While Discord remains available for those who choose to comply with the ID requirement, we recognize that many will not. Our Matrix, Stoat, and Root platforms provide full-featured alternatives where you can get support, report bugs, request features, and participate in our community—all without sacrificing your privacy.

Help Us Bridge These Platforms: We're looking for community members with experience in chat bridging solutions to help us connect Discord, Matrix, Stoat, and Root messages seamlessly. If you have expertise in this area and would like to contribute, please contact us at developers@acreetionos.org.
